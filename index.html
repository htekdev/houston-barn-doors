Signature product

Concealed ghost doors — architecture, not hardware.

A ghost door reads as part of the wall until you touch it. No visible frame, no visible track — just a clean line where a door used to be. It's the quietest way to make a room feel bigger, more modern, and unmistakably custom.

  • ◇

    Reads like art

    Panels the same plane as your wall. No frame, no reveal, no distraction — just a clean architectural statement.

Why homeowners choose them

Beautiful, functional, and a little bit magic.

Concealed ghost doors solve a real design problem — they give a room accessibility and openness without the visual noise of a traditional door. When they're closed, the room finishes cleaner. When they're open, they disappear against the wall.

  • → More usable square footage. Slides against the wall — no swing radius eating into your floor plan.
  • → Better accessibility. Wider clear openings with less effort than a swinging door.
  • → Architectural statement. A finished detail that upgrades the whole room — often the first thing guests comment on.
  • → Made for high-end homes. Built for clients who care about the small things — the ones you notice up close.
